Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba, or simply Demon Slayer, is a dark fantasy anime and manga that has taken the world by storm. The story centers on Tanjiro Kamado, a boy thrust into a life of demon slaying after his family is murdered and his sister Nezuko transformed into a demon. Tanjiro fights not only to avenge his family but also to find a cure for his sister, venturing on a thrilling journey filled with action, emotional depth, and the fight between good and evil. With stunning visuals and a unique blend of historical Japan and dark fantasy, Demon Slayer offers a captivating experience for audiences worldwide.

“The strong should aid and protect the weak. Then, the weak will become strong, and they in turn will aid and protect those weaker than them. That is the law of nature.” - Tanjiro Kamado
